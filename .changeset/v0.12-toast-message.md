Add the transient-feedback family: an imperative `toast` + `message` service with a single auto-mounted toaster. Toasts support tonal surfaces (info/success/warning/danger + high-contrast danger + custom brand-family/hex tones), a perimeter countdown timer (track/soft), Sonner-style stacking, six locations, actions, swipe-to-dismiss, `toast.promise`, grouping for messages, and a full WCAG-2.2 a11y contract. Ships Angular (`provideTulparToast`, `TulparToast`/`TulparMessage`, `<tulpar-toast-ng>`) and Vue (`useTulparToast`/`useTulparMessage`, `<TulparToast>`) wrappers.
